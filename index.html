CONCERN 01

Does it get too hot?

We won’t pretend any surface stays cool in full desert sun — and neither does US Turf. Our Platinum line adds thermo-cooling technology, and we design each lawn with shade, turf choice and smart material combos so it stays comfortable to use.

Does it drain when it rains?

Artificial grass is permeable and lets water flow right through. Under every lawn we install a compacted gravel sub-base engineered to keep water from pooling — so it drains, not puddles.

Permeable + gravel sub-base

How long will it last?

With proper care, our artificial grass can last up to 25 years. It’s backed by a 15-year manufacturer warranty on the turf plus a 2-year labor warranty — pet lines carry a 3-year manufacturer warranty.

Is it really zero maintenance?

We won’t say ‘maintenance-free’ — that’s misleading. It’s low maintenance: no mowing, fertilizing, irrigation or weed treatments. Just an occasional rinse and a brush in high-traffic spots.
